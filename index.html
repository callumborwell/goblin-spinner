Instructions

Adding Goblins

After typing a donator's name into the top textbox you can
      Add a basic goblin for $2
    OR
      Gamble for a little more on the wheel spin chance for a more powerful goblin
Where do the goblins go? Into the horde
    You can check the horde as it grows by clicking "Check Horde"

Unleash the Horde

The goblins pounce on the adventurers when someone donates to become their leader, or if they fumble into the open because the horde grew too large

Leading the Horde

    Once you believe the horde has grown large enough, you can donate bigger bucks to realease it and make it yours.
      Goblin Brute - bigger stronger goblin
      Worg Rider - goblin astride a vicious wolf-like creature
      Goblin Mage - dangerously explosive magic wielder
    When you do this, the goblins have successfully ambushed the party and get to strike first.

OR - Fumbled Check

As the horde grows, waiting for the right time to ambush, it could be exposed early by a bumbling goblin.
    Whenever a goblin is added, by button or wheel spin:
      1. Increase the horde count by one
      2. Roll a 100-sided die for the Fumble Check
      3. If the Fumble Check roll is greater than the horde count, the horde is still hidden
    BUT
      4. If the Fumble Check is less than or equal to the horde count, the horde is triggered
      4a. The goblin that caused the fumble get to be the leader
      4b. A fumbled horde goes after the adventurers

The Goblin List Tip

When the goblin list is visible, you can click on a goblin to strike through it and mark it as dead
